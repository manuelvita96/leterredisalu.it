Le Terre di Salu

Super Tuscan

The grapes harvested on the clayey hills of Vinci are carefully selected by hand to make this Super Tuscan a superlative wine.
A meeting between passion and dedication, this red is dressed in tenacity and elegance. Of an intense purple with garnet reflections, it presents itself to the nose with spicy and jam notes. Persistent and harmonious to the touch. Ideal with meat and cheese dishes. A wine suitable for aging and meditation.
