Partnership with Manvitech

OTT 18, 2021

The world of wine merges with that of information technology.
Who said that two apparently different worlds cannot find a meeting point?
Through this partnership it is made known that the passion for producing wine and the determination to program a website travel on different tracks but in the same direction. Our collaboration has solid foundations, built over the years, which is why we have entrusted the construction of our website and its continuous maintenance to Manvitech. Together with their professionalism and step by step attention we have developed a website on a simple but competitive technology, which has faithfully and honestly reproduced the online ecosystem of Le Terre di Salu. It is very important for us to take care of the image of our products and services, thanks to this precious collaboration we are happy to make our online cellar comfortable and at the same time professional.
